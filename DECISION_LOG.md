# Decision Log

## PURPOSE

Running record of every consequential decision. Maintained by the Scribe based on structured notifications from all agents. "Consequential" = affects direction, scope, methodology, or interpretation.

**MAINTAINED BY:** Scribe (primarily). COO may append directly for PI Checkpoint decisions.

---

## Entry Format

```
### [DATE] — Phase [N].[Step] — [Short Title]

**Decision:** [What was decided — one sentence]
**Alternatives considered:** [Other options]
**Rationale:** [Why this — 2-3 sentences max]
**Agent:** [Who made/recommended this decision]
**PI Involved:** [Yes/No — Yes for all PI Checkpoints]
**Risks accepted:** [What could go wrong]
**Reversibility:** [Can this be undone? At what cost?]
```
