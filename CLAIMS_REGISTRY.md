# Claims Registry

## PURPOSE

Tracks every empirical claim the project depends on or produces. Each entry has inline provenance: who added it, who verified it, when. The Librarian adds entries. The COO spot-checks and promotes to verified. The PM audits for staleness.

---

## Verification Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| UNVERIFIED | Recorded but not checked against a primary source |
| VERIFIED | Checked against primary source and confirmed accurate |
| PARTIALLY_VERIFIED | Source exists but details differ (see notes) |
| FAILED | Checked and found inaccurate, fabricated, or mischaracterized |
| UNABLE_TO_VERIFY | Primary source could not be located |
